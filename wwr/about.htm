Source of the Nile Rafting

At Source of the Nile Rafting, we live for the rush of whitewater and the joy of sharing it with you. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or a nature lover, our expert team is here to make your rafting adventure unforgettable.

History

Founded in the heart of Jinja, Uganda, our company has been guiding adventurers since 2010. From humble beginnings, we've grown to become the premier rafting company on the Nile, known for safety, professionalism, and fun!
