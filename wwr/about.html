Our History

Founded by Reagan Otema, Rafting Adventure is based at the Source of the Nile in Jinja, Uganda. With years of experience leading thrilling white-water rafting excursions, we’re dedicated to sharing the exhilarating beauty of Uganda’s rapids with the world. Our mission is to offer unforgettable adventures that combine excitement, safety, and a deep respect for nature.
