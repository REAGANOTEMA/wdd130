Source of the Nile Rafting

At Source of the Nile Rafting, we live for the rush of whitewater and the joy of sharing it with you. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or a nature lover, our expert team is here to make your rafting adventure unforgettable.

Our History

Founded in the heart of Jinja, Uganda, our company has been guiding adventurers since 2010. What began as a small group of passionate river lovers with just one raft and a dream has grown into a full-fledged adventure company known far beyond the banks of the Nile.

Our founders were inspired by the incredible natural beauty of the Nile and the joy it brought to every visitor. They believed that adventure should be accessible, safe, and absolutely unforgettable. Over the years, we have welcomed thousands of thrill-seekers from around the globe, sharing laughter, adrenaline, and deep respect for the river.

Today, we remain committed to the same principles we started with — safety, professionalism, and fun. Our experienced guides, top-notch equipment, and deep love for the Nile ensure every rafting trip is more than just an activity — it’s a life-changing experience.
