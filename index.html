About Me

Hello! I'm Reagan Otema, the founder and owner of Source of the Nile Rafting, based in Jinja, Uganda. What began as a personal passion for the river and adventure has grown into a professional company that offers world-class whitewater rafting experiences at the origin of the Nile River. I’m committed to combining adrenaline-filled journeys with top safety standards and memorable service.

With over a decade of experience navigating the Nile’s thrilling rapids, I’ve built a team that shares my passion for nature, community, and unforgettable outdoor exploration. Join us and discover the beauty and power of the Nile!

Uganda – Home of the Nile

Uganda, the Pearl of Africa, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. It’s home to the world’s second-largest freshwater lake — Lake Victoria — and the mighty Nile River, which begins its journey right here in Jinja. From lush green hills to roaring rapids, Uganda is a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers alike.
